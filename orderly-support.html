Help & Support

Answers to common questions — and a direct line to us if you need more

Getting started
How do I share my order page with customers? ▼
Go to your dashboard and click "Copy my order link" at the top. This copies a link that you can share anywhere — WhatsApp, Instagram bio, Facebook, or even just send directly to your regulars. When a customer opens the link, they see your store and can place an order immediately.
How do I add items to my menu? ▼
From your dashboard, scroll down to the "My Menu" section on the right side. Type the item name and price, then click "+ Add". Your menu updates instantly — customers will see the new item the next time they open your order page.
How do I set up my store details and welcome message? ▼
Go to Settings from the left sidebar. There you can update your store name, city, food type, welcome message, order deadline, delivery settings, and more. Everything saves with one click.
Orders
How do I know when I get a new order? ▼
The moment a customer places an order, Orderly opens a WhatsApp message pre-filled with all their order details — their name, items, total, and delivery preference. You just hit send in WhatsApp. You'll also see the order appear on your dashboard immediately.
How do I update a customer on their order status? ▼
On your dashboard, click "Update status" next to any order. You'll see the next steps in the flow — Confirmed, Preparing, Packing, Out for delivery, Delivered. Each time you update, Orderly opens a WhatsApp message to that customer with a personal update. You just hit send.
Can I cancel an order? ▼
Yes. Click "Update status" on the order, then scroll to "Cancel this order instead" at the bottom. You'll pick a reason (too short notice, ingredient unavailable, etc.) and Orderly sends an automatic WhatsApp apology to the customer with the reason included.
How do I message multiple customers at once? ▼
On your dashboard, click "Select multiple" at the top of your orders list. Tap the orders you want to message, then click "💬 Send message" in the bar that appears at the bottom. Choose a template — like "Your order is ready for collection" or "Small delay" — and Orderly walks you through sending to each customer on WhatsApp one by one.
Invoices
How do I send an invoice to a customer? ▼
Click the "🧾 Invoice" button on any order row. A preview of the invoice appears — then click "Send on WhatsApp" to send it directly to that customer, or "Download PDF" to save it.
Where can I see all my past invoices? ▼
Go to Invoices from the left sidebar. You'll see every invoice in order, with the customer name, amount, and status. Free plan shows your 3 most recent — upgrade to Pro for your full history.
Plan & billing
What's included in my free trial? ▼
Your first 30 days give you full Pro access — unlimited orders, unlimited menu items, full invoice history, and no Orderly branding on your order page. No credit card required. After 30 days you can continue on the Free plan or upgrade to Pro.
What are the Free plan limits? ▼
The Free plan includes up to 50 orders per month and up to 20 menu items. Your last 3 invoices are visible. Orderly branding appears on your order page and invoices. All core features — order management, WhatsApp notifications, status tracking — remain fully available.
What does Pro include? ▼
Pro is R199/month or R2,000/year (saves you R388). It includes unlimited orders, unlimited menu items, your own store branding on the order page and invoices, full invoice history, and manual invoice creation for cash/offline sales. Online payments and full analytics are coming soon for Pro users.
Can I get a refund on a yearly subscription? ▼
Yearly subscriptions are non-refundable if cancelled before the year expires. You'll keep full Pro access until the end of your paid year. If you have any concerns, please reach out to us at hellofromorderly@gmail.com — we're always happy to help.
Account
How do I reset my password? ▼
Go to the sign in page and click "Forgot password?". Enter your email and we'll send you a reset link. Check your inbox — including your spam folder just in case.
How do I delete my store? ▼
Go to Settings and scroll to the Danger Zone at the bottom. Click "Delete my store" and confirm by typing your store name. This permanently deletes all your data — orders, menu items, and your account. This cannot be undone.
